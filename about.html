About GreenSnow

Welcome to GreenSnow, where we're dedicated to keeping your property looking its best no matter the season. We specialize in creating lush, healthy lawns during the warmer months and ensuring your driveways and walkways are clear and safe during the winter. Our story began with a simple goal: to help homeowners enjoy their outdoor spaces without the hassle of constant maintenance. What started as a small, family-operated service quickly grew into a trusted name in lawn care and snow removal. Today, we’re proud to offer year-round services that are tailored to meet the unique needs of our community.

Our Mission

At GreenSnow, we're committed to making your life easier. From delivering precise lawn care that boosts your curb appeal to tackling even the toughest snowstorms, we work tirelessly to ensure your property is pristine, safe, and enjoyable.

Our Journey

Our journey began years ago when we noticed a gap in reliable seasonal property services. We saw homeowners struggling with overgrown lawns in the summer and hazardous icy driveways in the winter. Armed with a love for the outdoors, the right tools, and a passion for helping others, we set out to create a solution. Through hard work, attention to detail, and a focus on customer satisfaction, GreenSnow became the go-to service for year-round property care. What sets us apart is our dedication to building relationships: we don't just maintain properties; we take pride in making our customers’ lives easier and their homes more beautiful.

Why Choose Us?

  • Professional Expertise: Our exerienced team is trained in the latest techniques for both lawn care and snow or removal.
  • Reliable service: Were there when you need us rain shine or snowstorm.
  • Co-friendly Approach: We use subtainable practices to protect your lawn and environment.
  • Customer Focused: Your satisfaction is our top priority.

Let us handle the hard work, so you can enjoy every season without worry. Whether it's mowing your lawn, creating a lush landscape, or clearing snow after a winter storm, GreenSnow is here to help.
